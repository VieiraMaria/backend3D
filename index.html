About Percy Jackson

Percy Jackson is a fictional character, the title character and narrator of Rick Riordan's Percy Jackson & the Olympians series. He is also one of seven main protagonists of the sequel series The Heroes of Olympus, appearing in every book except The Lost Hero, and appears in the ongoing Trials of Apollo series, making him one of the few characters to appear in all three series of the Camp Half-Blood chronicles.

Throughout the series, Percy Jackson grows from a gawky, introverted preteen boy to a powerful young man with a strong sense of responsibility to his friends, family, and the world around him. He is known for his bravery, loyalty, and his unique ability to control water.

The Percy Jackson series has gained widespread popularity among young adult readers and has been adapted into movies and graphic novels.
